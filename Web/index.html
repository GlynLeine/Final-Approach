What is Android Assembly?

In Android Asesembly you get to take charge at the control panel of a robot building factory. Watching the conveyor belt move and controlling the insides of the factory using the console, you get to play around with physics and get a better understanding of how the products and items you use every day get created.


The process starts by stepping up to the console, there you press the big button to start the assembly line. This will start the mechanical chain reaction, showing the automatic assembly line as it brings the parts together. As soon as they're together the console lights up and you have to guide the parts through the factory to build your very own robot! Once it's done, you'll watch him get shipped.

Visit Android Assembly at the maker festival if you want to take control of an assembly line and make a robot as well!
